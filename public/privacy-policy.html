Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 17, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the Haryana CET Group C Result Guide ("we", "our", or "the site") collects, uses, and shares information when you visit our website.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personally identifiable information (PII) directly. When you use the result search feature, you enter a roll number or registration number — this is sent as a query to our server solely to retrieve publicly released HSSC CET result data. We do not store, log, or retain individual search queries against any personal profile.

We may collect anonymised, aggregated usage data such as page views, browser type, and general geographic region through analytics tools to improve the service.

2. Cookies

This site uses cookies in the following ways:

Service Worker cache: A browser-side service worker caches static assets (HTML, fonts) to improve page load speed. This does not transmit any data to our servers.

Google AdSense: We use Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google may use cookies to show you relevant ads based on your interests. You can opt out of personalised advertising by visiting adssettings.google.com.

Analytics: We may use Google Analytics or similar tools to collect anonymised visitor statistics. These tools may set cookies on your device.

3. Advertising

We use Google AdSense, a third-party advertising service provided by Google LLC, to serve advertisements on this site. Google AdSense uses cookies to personalise ads based on your prior visits to this and other websites. Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visit to our site and/or other sites on the internet.

You may opt out of personalised advertising at google.com/settings/ads. For more information, see Google's advertising policies.

4. Third-Party Links

This site contains links to external websites including hssc.gov.in, haryanaroj.nic.in, and others. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those external sites.

5. Data Accuracy Disclaimer

The result data displayed on this portal is based on publicly released HSSC CET Group C 2025 data. The ranks shown are indicative only and are not official. We are not affiliated with, endorsed by, or connected to the Haryana Staff Selection Commission (HSSC) or any government body.

6. Children's Privacy

This site does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13 years of age.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

8. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you may reach us via the GitHub repository or the contact information listed on the official HSSC portal for data concerns.
